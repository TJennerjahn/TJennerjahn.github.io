Welcome!

This is my little corner of the Internet.

I mostly write about technology, workflow improvement and personal development, but I'll occasionally weigh in on my thoughts about the state of the world.

There are many things that I don't like about the modern Internet, one of them being that everyone feels the need to share their every opinion. There I'll carefully consider if what I have to say actualy adds anything new to the conversation.

Another thing that I find incredibly annoying is the amount of Javascript that is apparently needed on many sites just to display a few paragraphs of text. In an effort to show that the web is perfectly usable without a concoction of frameworks, plugins and ad tracking, I built this site without any of that. Hopefully this has made for a snappy experience regardless of your device and/or internet connection.

Please enjoy your stay.
