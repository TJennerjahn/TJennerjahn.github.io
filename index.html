Welcome!

This is my little corner of the Internet.

I haven't published a lot of things here. In the past I've held myself to a standard that I couldn't achieve for an audience that doesn't exist

So going forward I'll just post whatever I want whenever I feel like it. I'm glad if you enjoy some of it. If you don't... Oh well.

One thing I find incredibly annoying is the amount of Javascript that is apparently needed on many sites just to display a few paragraphs of text. In an effort to show that the web is perfectly usable without a concoction of frameworks, plugins and ad tracking, I built this site without any of that. Hopefully this has made for a snappy experience regardless of your device and/or internet connection.

Please enjoy your stay.
